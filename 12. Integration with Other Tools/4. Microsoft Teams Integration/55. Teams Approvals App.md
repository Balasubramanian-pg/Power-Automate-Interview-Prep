# 55. Teams Approvals App

Canonical documentation for 55. Teams Approvals App. This document defines the conceptual model, terminology, and standard usage patterns.

> [!NOTE]
> This documentation is implementation-agnostic and intended to serve as a stable reference.

## 1. Purpose and Problem Space
The 55. Teams Approvals App exists to streamline and simplify the approval process within Microsoft Teams, addressing the class of problems related to manual, time-consuming, and often disorganized approval workflows. This app aims to increase productivity, reduce errors, and enhance collaboration among team members by providing a centralized and structured approach to requesting and granting approvals.

## 2. Conceptual Overview
The 55. Teams Approvals App is designed to integrate seamlessly with Microsoft Teams, allowing users to create, manage, and track approval requests directly within the Teams platform. The app provides a user-friendly interface for requesting approvals, assigning approvers, and setting deadlines, as well as features for tracking the status of approval requests and receiving notifications when approvals are granted or rejected.

## 3. Terminology and Definitions
| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Approval Request | A formal request for approval submitted through the 55. Teams Approvals App. |
| Approver | A user designated to review and grant or reject an approval request. |
| Requester | The user who submits an approval request. |
| Approval Template | A pre-defined template used to create approval requests with specific settings and configurations. |
| Custom Approval | An approval request created with unique settings and configurations, outside of a standard template. |

## 4. Core Concepts
The 55. Teams Approvals App is built around several core concepts, including:
- **Approval Requests**: The foundation of the app, allowing users to submit requests for approval.
- **Approval Workflows**: The structured process by which approval requests are routed to approvers and tracked.
- **Templates and Custom Approvals**: Providing flexibility in creating approval requests, whether through pre-defined templates or custom configurations.
- **Integration with Microsoft Teams**: Enhancing the Teams experience by bringing approval processes directly into the platform.

## 5. Standard Model
The standard model for using the 55. Teams Approvals App involves the following steps:
1. **Installation and Setup**: Adding the app to Microsoft Teams and configuring basic settings.
2. **Creating Approval Requests**: Using templates or custom configurations to submit requests for approval.
3. **Assigning Approvers**: Designating users to review and act on approval requests.
4. **Tracking and Notifications**: Monitoring the status of approval requests and receiving updates on approvals or rejections.

## 6. Common Patterns
Common patterns of usage include:
- **Regular Approval Requests**: Submitting approval requests on a recurring basis, such as for expense reports or project proposals.
- **Ad-Hoc Approvals**: Creating custom approval requests for unique situations or one-time needs.
- **Team-Based Approvals**: Using the app to manage approvals within specific teams or channels in Microsoft Teams.

## 7. Anti-Patterns
Discouraged practices include:
- **Overly Complex Approval Workflows**: Creating approval processes that are too intricate or difficult to follow.
- **Insufficient Communication**: Failing to clearly define the purpose or requirements of an approval request.
- **Inconsistent Use**: Not adhering to established approval templates or processes, leading to confusion or errors.
