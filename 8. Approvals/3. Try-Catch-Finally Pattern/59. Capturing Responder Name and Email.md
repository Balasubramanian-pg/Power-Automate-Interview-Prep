## 1. Purpose and Problem Space
Describe why 59. Capturing Responder Name and Email exists and the class of problems it addresses.
The purpose of capturing responder name and email is to collect and store the identity and contact information of individuals who respond to surveys, forms, or other data collection instruments. This addresses the problem of anonymity in data collection, allowing for follow-up communications, data validation, and personalized interactions. The class of problems it addresses includes respondent tracking, data quality control, and targeted communication.

## 2. Conceptual Overview

Capturing responder name and email involves a simple data collection process where respondents voluntarily provide their name and email address, typically through a web form or survey instrument. This information is then stored in a database or data management system, enabling subsequent analysis, tracking, and communication.

The standard model for capturing responder name and email involves a web-based data collection instrument, such as a survey or form, that includes fields for responders to input their name and email address.
