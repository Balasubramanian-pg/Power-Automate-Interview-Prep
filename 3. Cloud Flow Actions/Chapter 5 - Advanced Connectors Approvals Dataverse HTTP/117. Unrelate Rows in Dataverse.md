## 1. Purpose and Problem Space
Describe why 117. Unrelate Rows in Dataverse exists and the class of problems it addresses.
The purpose of unrelating rows in Dataverse is to remove associations between records, allowing for data management and organization. This feature addresses the problem of unnecessary relationships between data entities, which can lead to data inconsistencies, reduced performance, and decreased data quality. By unrelating rows, users can maintain data integrity, improve data governance, and enhance overall system efficiency.

## 2. Conceptual Overview
Provide a high-level mental model of the topic.
Unrelating rows in Dataverse involves disconnecting the relationships between two or more records, effectively removing the link between them. This process can be performed manually or programmatically, depending on the specific use case and requirements. The conceptual model involves understanding the relationships between data entities, identifying the relationships to be removed, and executing the unrelate operation.
